WHAT IS HOOP STUDIO

Hoop Studio is a creative team primarily focused on game development with a strong portfolio of games, some of which have won national awards.

At Hoop Studio, we blend technical expertise with creativity, aiming to bring innovative concepts to life across multiple digital platforms.
